# MTG Tarot Minor Arcana — Green Suits (Deduplicated)

## Overview

This document contains card selections for two Minor Arcana suits:
- **Groves (GW)** from the Sephirothic Engine — Green-White allied pair
- **Grafts (GU)** from the Qliphothic Engine — Green-Blue enemy pair

This pairing shows how Green functions differently in cooperation (with White) vs opposition (against Blue).

**Color Requirement:** All cards must be the suit's color pair — either multicolor GW/GU, or hybrid mana cards in those colors.

**Deduplication Rule:** Each card appears in exactly ONE slot per suit. No duplicates.

# Comparison Notes

## How Green Functions Differently

**In Groves (GW — Cooperation):**
Green's growth combines with White's community to create *natural order* — the garden tended together, strength in numbers, growth through cooperation. The cards emphasize:
- Token generation and population
- Lifegain from creatures
- Convoke and collective effects
- Protection through numbers
- Community as ecosystem

**In Grafts (GU — Opposition):**
Green's growth grinds against Blue's artifice to create *evolution* — nature modified, growth through experimentation, the tension between natural and artificial. The cards emphasize:
- +1/+1 counter manipulation
- Card draw from growth
- Creatures with spell-like abilities
- Flash and counter magic on creatures
- Nature enhanced beyond natural limits
