# MTG Tarot Minor Arcana — Second Two Suits (Deduplicated)

## Overview

This document contains card selections for two Minor Arcana suits:
- **Secrets (UB)** from the Sephirothic Engine — Blue-Black allied pair
- **Sparks (UR)** from the Qliphothic Engine — Blue-Red enemy pair

**Color Requirement:** All cards must be the suit's color pair — either multicolor UB/UR, or hybrid mana cards in those colors.

**Deduplication Rule:** Each card appears in exactly ONE slot per suit. No duplicates.

# Comparison Notes

## How Blue Functions Differently

**In Secrets (UB — Cooperation):**
Blue's knowledge combines with Black's ambition to create *asymmetry* — knowing what others don't, power through hidden information. The cards emphasize:
- Information theft and extraction
- Unblockable/infiltrating creatures
- Graveyard as information archive
- Opponents' resources becoming yours
- Patience and inevitability

**In Sparks (UR — Opposition):**
Blue's intellect grinds against Red's passion to create *volatility* — genius that might explode, creativity on the edge. The cards emphasize:
- Spells triggering spells triggering damage
- Haste + card advantage (impatient knowledge)
- Copying and amplification
- Power from accumulated spellcasting
- Breakthrough or breakdown
