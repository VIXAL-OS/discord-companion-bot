# MTG Tarot Minor Arcana — Red Suits (Deduplicated)

## Overview

This document contains card selections for two Minor Arcana suits:
- **Wilds (RG)** from the Sephirothic Engine — Red-Green allied pair
- **Crusades (RW)** from the Qliphothic Engine — Red-White enemy pair

This pairing shows how Red functions differently in cooperation (with Green) vs opposition (against White).

**Color Requirement:** All cards must be the suit's color pair — either multicolor RG/RW, or hybrid mana cards in those colors.

**Deduplication Rule:** Each card appears in exactly ONE slot per suit. No duplicates.

# Comparison Notes

## How Red Functions Differently

**In Wilds (RG — Cooperation):**
Red's passion combines with Green's nature to create *primal fury* — instinct honored, strength through directness, letting nature decide. The cards emphasize:
- Haste and trample together
- Creatures that grow from attacking
- Punishment for noncreature spells
- Mana acceleration into bigger creatures
- The wisdom of savagery

**In Crusades (RW — Opposition):**
Red's passion grinds against White's order to create *disciplined fury* — fire constrained into blade, warfare as doctrine, the tension between freedom and structure. The cards emphasize:
- Mentor and training mechanics
- Extra combat and battalion synergies
- Protection spells that enable aggression
- Angels leading armies
- The cost of organization
