# MTG Tarot Minor Arcana — First Two Suits

## Overview

This document contains card selections for two Minor Arcana suits:
- **Edicts (WU)** from the Sephirothic Engine — White-Blue allied pair
- **Debts (WB)** from the Qliphothic Engine — White-Black enemy pair

This pairing lets us see how White functions differently in cooperation (with Blue) versus opposition (against Black).

**Color Requirement:** All cards must be the suit's color pair — either multicolor WU/WB, or hybrid mana cards in those colors.

# Comparison Notes

## How White Functions Differently

**In Edicts (WU — Cooperation):**
White's order combines with Blue's knowledge to create *systems* — laws, institutions, codes that persist and function. The cards emphasize:
- Prevention over punishment
- Information and foresight
- Systematic enforcement
- Legacy through structure

**In Debts (WB — Opposition):**
White's order grinds against Black's ambition to create *costs* — moral compromises, pragmatic choices, debts that accumulate. The cards emphasize:
- Payment and extraction
- Life as currency
- Sacrifice for power
- Legacy through what you owe
