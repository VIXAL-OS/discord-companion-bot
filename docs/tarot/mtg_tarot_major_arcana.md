# MTG Tarot Major Arcana — Verified Card Selections

## Overview

This document contains the Major Arcana selections for both the **Sephirothic Engine** (Path of Harmony) and **Qliphothic Engine** (Path of Shells). All cards have been verified for color identity accuracy.

**Color Requirement:** All cards must be colorless (artifacts, Eldrazi, colorless planeswalkers)
