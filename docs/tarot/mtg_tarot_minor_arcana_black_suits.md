# MTG Tarot Minor Arcana — Black Suits (Deduplicated)

## Overview

This document contains card selections for two Minor Arcana suits:
- **Revelry (BR)** from the Sephirothic Engine — Black-Red allied pair
- **Rot (BG)** from the Qliphothic Engine — Black-Green enemy pair

This pairing shows how Black functions differently in cooperation (with Red) vs opposition (against Green).

**Color Requirement:** All cards must be the suit's color pair — either multicolor BR/BG, or hybrid mana cards in those colors.

**Deduplication Rule:** Each card appears in exactly ONE slot per suit. No duplicates.

# Comparison Notes

## How Black Functions Differently

**In Revelry (BR — Cooperation):**
Black's ambition combines with Red's passion to create *liberation through destruction* — freedom found in excess, chains broken through hedonism. The cards emphasize:
- Damage and discard together
- Creatures that benefit from opponent pain
- Sacrifice as party trick
- Living fast, dying gloriously
- Carnival chaos

**In Rot (BG — Opposition):**
Black's death focus grinds against Green's life focus to create *cycles* — death feeding life, necessary endings enabling new growth. The cards emphasize:
- Graveyard as resource
- Creatures returning from death
- Sacrifice feeding growth
- Patient accumulation
- The long view of decay
